[🦡Skunk](https://www.github.com/gearfactory/Skunk) 是一个基于Reactor利用C++编写的网络通信框架，Skunk将会在下面的Gear Factory项目中被使用：

* [Crab RPC通信框架](https://github.com/gearfactory/Crab-CXX)
* [Spider Raft共识协议库](https://www.github.com/Spider)
* [Giraffe 分布式协调组件](https://github.com/gearfactory/Giraffe)
* [Fox 简单的HTTP服务器](https://github.com/gearfactory/Fox)
* [Owl 分布式调度系统](https://github.com/gearfactory/Owl)
* [Squirrel 分布式关系型数据库](https://github.com/gearfactory/Squirrel)
* [Camel 分布式KV存储](https://github.com/gearfactory/Camel)
* [Monkey 分布式搜索引擎](https://github.com/gearfactory/Monkey)
* [Octopus 分布式网络爬虫](https://github.com/gearfactory/Octopus)
